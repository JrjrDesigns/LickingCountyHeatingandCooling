HVAC Tune-Up & Maintenance in Licking County, Ohio

Annual HVAC maintenance is the single best thing you can do to prevent expensive breakdowns and keep your energy bills low. We provide furnace and air conditioning tune-ups throughout Licking County — Newark, Heath, Granville, Pataskala, Utica, Buckeye Lake, Hebron, Johnstown, and New Albany.

Our comprehensive tune-up includes a full inspection of your heating or cooling system, cleaning of key components, lubrication of moving parts, refrigerant check, and a written summary of findings. We'll let you know if anything looks like it's heading toward failure — before it becomes an emergency.

Signs You Need HVAC Tune-Up

  • You haven't had a tune-up in over a year
  • Your energy bills are higher than usual
  • Your system is making new sounds
  • You want to get ahead of the heating or cooling season
  • Your warranty requires annual maintenance
  • You're preparing to sell your home
  • Your system is 5+ years old
  • You want documentation of your HVAC system's condition
