Furnace Repair in Licking County, Ohio

A broken furnace in an Ohio winter isn't just uncomfortable — it's a health and safety issue. Our technicians are available 24/7 for furnace repair throughout Licking County, with same-day service in Newark, Heath, Granville, Pataskala, Utica, Hebron, Johnstown, Buckeye Lake, and New Albany.

We diagnose and repair all types of furnace problems: failed igniters, cracked heat exchangers, faulty blower motors, dirty flame sensors, and more. We work on all major brands including Carrier, Lennox, Trane, Rheem, Bryant, York, and American Standard.

Signs You Need Furnace Repair

  • Furnace blowing cold air
  • Furnace not turning on
  • Strange banging, rattling, or squealing sounds
  • Furnace short-cycling (turning on and off repeatedly)
  • Yellow or orange pilot light instead of blue
  • Unusually high heating bills
  • Uneven heat throughout the home
  • Furnace running constantly without reaching set temperature
