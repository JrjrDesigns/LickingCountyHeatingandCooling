Concrete & Paving Work in Hebron

Hebron's proximity to Buckeye Lake brings a slightly different mix of projects than the rest of the county — properties that were originally built as seasonal lake cottages and have since been converted to year-round homes, often with driveways and walkways that were never built to the same standard as a typical residential build.

Village-lot properties in Hebron also tend to run smaller and tighter than newer subdivision lots elsewhere in the county, which changes how we approach equipment access and staging for a pour or paving job. We plan access before scheduling, not after showing up.

"A fair number of the Hebron driveways we look at started life as a gravel lane to a lake cottage that got paved over at some point without much base work, because it was never meant to carry daily vehicle traffic. When a seasonal property becomes a full-time home, the driveway often needs to be rebuilt properly rather than patched, and we tell people that honestly rather than just resurfacing over a problem."
