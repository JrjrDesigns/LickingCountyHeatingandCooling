Concrete & Paving Work in Heath

Heath's character splits pretty evenly between residential ranch-style neighborhoods built mid-century and the retail and light-industrial development along State Route 79 and near the airport industrial park. That mix means we do a lot of both residential driveway work and commercial paving in Heath — parking lots, pads, and loading areas for the businesses along the commercial corridor.

Commercial customers in Heath are usually working around live business hours, which changes how we plan a project. We phase larger lots in sections and schedule major pours for slower periods where possible, so a retail or industrial property doesn't have to shut down completely during paving.

"Heath is where we do the most commercial concrete and asphalt work in the county. The lots along 79 see real daily truck traffic, so we build the base and asphalt lift thickness for that use, not just for passenger cars. We've seen too many commercial lots elsewhere get paved to residential specs and start rutting within a year under delivery truck weight."
