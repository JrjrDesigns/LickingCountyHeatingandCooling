Who We Are

HVAC Service Built for Licking County

Licking County Heating and Cooling connects homeowners across Newark, Heath, Granville, Pataskala, Utica, Buckeye Lake, Hebron, Johnstown, and New Albany with local, licensed HVAC contractors who know this area.

We're not a national company with a Columbus call center routing you to whoever is available. We focus exclusively on Licking County — which means faster response times, technicians who know local housing stock, and real accountability to the community we serve.

Our network of HVAC professionals handles everything from emergency furnace repair on the coldest nights of the year to full system installations and annual tune-ups. Every technician we work with is licensed, insured, and background-checked. See our full range of services.
