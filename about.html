Our Approach

Base First. Everything Else Follows.

We pour concrete and lay asphalt across Licking County — driveways, patios, walkways, and commercial lots and pads. Our approach starts underground: proper excavation depth, compacted aggregate base, and correct joint or lift planning before any concrete or asphalt goes down. That's the part of the job nobody sees once it's finished, and it's the part that determines whether a driveway lasts a decade or three.

We work directly with homeowners on residential driveways, patios, and walkways, and with property managers and business owners on commercial parking lots, pads, and site concrete throughout Licking County — including Newark, Heath, Granville, Pataskala, Hebron, and Johnstown. Head back to our homepage anytime for the full service overview.

"We'd rather lose a bid to a lower price than win one by cutting the base. It's the single biggest factor in whether a customer calls us back in five years for more work, or calls us back in two years with a cracked driveway."
