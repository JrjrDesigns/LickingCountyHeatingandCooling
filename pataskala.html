Concrete & Paving Work in Pataskala

Pataskala's growth means we see a real mix of project types — new-construction driveways for homes still being finished out, larger asphalt driveways on bigger suburban lots, and increasingly, commercial paving as retail and light industrial development follows the residential growth into the area.

New-construction driveways in Pataskala come with their own base considerations: heavy construction equipment often compacts or disturbs soil unevenly around a new home before the driveway is ever poured, so we verify base conditions on new builds rather than assuming the site is ready just because the house is finished.

"Pataskala is one of the few places in the county where we regularly get called out before a house is even fully landscaped. New-construction sites look ready for a driveway pour, but heavy equipment traffic during the build can leave inconsistent compaction underneath. We test and prep the base properly rather than pouring straight onto whatever grade the builder left, which is a step some new-construction driveways skip."
