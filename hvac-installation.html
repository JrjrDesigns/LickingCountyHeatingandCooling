HVAC Installation in Licking County, Ohio

Whether your current system is beyond repair or you're building new, we install high-efficiency heating and cooling systems throughout Licking County. We serve Newark, Heath, Granville, Pataskala, Utica, Buckeye Lake, Hebron, Johnstown, and New Albany with no-pressure consultations and honest quotes.

A properly sized and installed HVAC system will run more efficiently, last longer, and keep your home more comfortable than an oversized or undersized unit. We do a full load calculation before recommending any equipment — we're not in the business of selling you more than you need.

Signs You Need HVAC Installation

  • System is 15+ years old
  • Repair costs are approaching half the price of replacement
  • Home is adding square footage or a new addition
  • Energy bills keep climbing year over year
  • System requires frequent repairs
  • Home has uneven temperatures room to room
  • Current system uses R-22 refrigerant (being phased out)
  • You're building a new home in Licking County
